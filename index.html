Jaeyoung Lee

PhD Candidate · Department of Statistics · Virginia Tech

I am a PhD Candidate in Department of Statistics at Virginia Tech working on variational deep generative latent modeling on heterogeneous data. My work sits at the intersection of Statistics and Machine learning, with a focus on developing variational generative models for structural learning on heterogeneous input data and robust prediction by linking latent space and resonses.

I am advised by Dr. Hongxiao Zhu at Virginia Tech.
